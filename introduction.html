Consent

What will happen with your data?

  • We will record your keyboard responses.
  • We will store no other data about you; including any personal data you have shared with or that is associated with Prolific, such as your name, Prolific ID, IP address, or browser type.
  • Your data will be stored for a minimum of three and a maximum of ten years, and made freely available via the University of Tromsø’s Open Research Data repository, in accordance with the research data management guidelines of UiT and the Norwegian Research Council.

Withdrawing from the study

In this study, you cannot be identified in the data material. This means we cannot withdraw your data from our dataset after you have completed the study. You can withdraw your participation at any point during the study, by clicking the ‘Quit’ button in the bottom right corner of your screen. All data we have collected from you will then be automatically deleted.

On behalf of UiT, NSD - Norwegian Center for Research Data AS has considered that the processing of personal data in this project is in accordance with the privacy regulations.

By beginning the study, you acknowledge that you have read this information and agree to participate, with the knowledge that you are free to withdraw your participation at any time.
